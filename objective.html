Objective

    1. Enhance Energy Efficiency:

    To develop a smart street lighting system that adjusts the brightness of street lights based on real-time data such as traffic flow, weather, and ambient light levels, reducing unnecessary energy consumption.


    1. Cost Reduction:

    To lower electricity and maintenance costs by using sensors to control the lighting and only keep the lights on when needed, ensuring optimal use of resources.


    1. Improve Public Safety:

    To provide brighter street lighting in areas with high traffic or pedestrian activity, while dimming or turning off lights in low-traffic areas, improving both safety and security


    1. Remote Control via App:

    To build a user-friendly mobile app that enables city officials or users to remotely control and monitor the street lighting system, providing convenience and quick access to management features.


    1. Integration of AI and IoT:

    To integrate AI and IoT technologies to automate lighting decisions, ensuring the system is adaptive and responsive to changing conditions in the environment.
